A Visual Archive of Nuclear Optimism

AVANO is an internet archive of images that demonstrate social and cultural optimism towards atomic and nuclear things. The growing collection of images are selected according to breadth of cultural impact and significance in historical and current times, and are placed together to form a natural display of — how, when and why are nuclear things advocated? The gallery of images produced as propaganda, advertisement, publicity and disinformation are thus reappropriated into an alarming account of nuclear history.
